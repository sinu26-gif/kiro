## 6. RISKS & CAVEATS

1. **LTP volatility risk** — BNHC's LTP could move 5–15% between today (May 28) and your bid day (June 12). Always recompute using the actual LTP on the morning of June 12.
2. **Oversubscription risk** — With 474,696 units and likely thousands of bidders, you may receive only a fraction of your 1,000 even if your bid wins. Plan for partial allotment.
3. **Outlier discount risk** — In 13% of comparable auctions, the cutoff cleared at >25% discount (e.g., BHL at 38.8%, DORDI's 1st auction at 29%). If BNHC turns into an outlier, NPR 285 means you overpay relative to cutoff. *However, you still get the shares — you just don't get the maximum possible profit.*
4. **Listing-day price drop risk** — Nothing guarantees the post-allotment price stays at NPR 338. If the broader market or hydropower index falls before listing, your "profit margin" shrinks or turns negative.
5. **Capital lock-up** — NPR 285,000 is a meaningful sum. It will be locked from bid day until either allotment+listing or refund (typically 7–15 business days). Make sure this is money you can afford to tie up.
6. **First-time applicant logistics** — Verify your DEMAT account is active, your linked bank account has cleared funds, and your broker's online portal supports auction bidding before June 4.
