NEPSE Auction-Share Analyzer

A complete browser-based research & quantitative system for NEPSE auction shares — from data ingest to event-window analysis, technicals, fundamentals, macro & budget overlays, Monte Carlo with Jump-Diffusion, mean-CVaR portfolio optimization, and a printable decision-grade report. Sector-agnostic: works for hydropower today, banking tomorrow, microfinance next month.

Auction Timeline

Step 3

Technical Analysis

SMA / EMA stack, RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands, ADX, volume profile and a -5 to +5 technical scorecard per ticker.

Open module →
Step 4

Fundamental Analysis

Common ratios vs sector medians, sector-specific KPIs, DCF + DDM intrinsic value (bear / base / bull) and a fundamental scorecard.

Open module →
Step 5

Macro & Budget

Nepal macro inputs (NRB rates, CPI, BoP) and a 3-scenario budget overlay (bullish / neutral / bearish) that feeds into Monte Carlo as μ/σ shocks.

Open module →
Step 6

Monte Carlo

GBM + Merton Jump-Diffusion with EWMA / GARCH(1,1) volatility, regime-switching across the 3 budget scenarios. Fan chart, terminal histogram, P(profit > floor bid), VaR & CVaR.

Open module →
Step 7

Portfolio Optimizer

Allocate your bid capital across the auction tickers using equal-weight, risk-parity, or mean-CVaR with random-search. Outputs NPR allocation and bid quantities.

Open module →
Step 8

Decision Report

One-page synthesis: per-ticker decision card, recommended bids, allocation, conviction tags and a risk register. Print-friendly.

Open module →
