# Himova — Wholesale Portal & Free POS for Shopkeepers

## One-Line Pitch
A B2B wholesale ordering portal where shopkeepers buy from Himova, and in return get a **free, full-featured POS system** to run their own retail shop — turning every customer into a long-term partner.

## The Problem
- Wholesalers in Nepal still take orders over WhatsApp and phone calls.
- There is no central record of who ordered what, when, or how much they sold.
- Shopkeepers lack affordable digital tools for stock and sales tracking.
- No data-driven decisions are possible for either side.

## The Solution
A single Progressive Web App (PWA) with two sides:

1. **Admin Portal (Himova)** — manage products, shopkeepers, orders, stock, leaderboard, and analytics from a desktop/laptop.
2. **Shopkeeper Portal** — mobile-first interface that lets each shopkeeper:
   - Order shoes/clothing in pre-defined sets from Himova.
   - Track everything they have bought and sold.
   - Use a built-in POS to run their daily retail business.
   - Compete on a public leaderboard for rewards.

## Why This Wins
- **Free POS = Loyalty.** Shopkeepers stay because their entire business runs on Himova's system.
- **Data lock-in.** Their stock, customers, and sales history live inside Himova.
- **Network effects.** Public leaderboard creates social proof and friendly competition.
- **Insights.** Himova learns what shoes sell where, and can stock smarter.

## Target Market (Phase 1)
- **Country:** Nepal only
- **Users:** Footwear and clothing shopkeepers (Himova's wholesale buyers)
- **Languages:** English + Nepali

## Business Model
- **For shopkeepers:** 100% free. No subscription, no hidden fees.
- **For Himova:** Revenue comes from product sales (the existing wholesale business). The platform increases retention and order volume.
- **Future:** White-label the system to other wholesalers (Phase 3).

## Core Differentiators
1. **Sets-based ordering** built into the data model (not retrofitted).
2. **Dual-stock model** — warehouse stock and shop stock tracked separately, linked automatically.
3. **Gamification** — leaderboard, badges, and manual rewards drive ordering behaviour.
4. **WhatsApp-first notifications** — meets shopkeepers where they already are, with zero SMS cost.
5. **Bilingual UI** — English/Nepali toggle on every screen.

## Phasing
| Phase | Timeline | Goal |
|---|---|---|
| **Phase 1 — MVP** | 1–2 months | Admin can list products, shopkeepers can order, basic POS works, leaderboard live. Real users using it. |
| **Phase 2 — Growth** | +2 months | Multi-branch, advanced gamification, expense tracking, deeper analytics, exports. |
| **Phase 3 — Scale** | Later | Multi-wholesaler marketplace, native mobile apps, loyalty programs. |

## Success Metrics (after Phase 1 launch)
- 20+ active shopkeepers ordering monthly through the portal.
- 80%+ of Himova's wholesale orders placed via the portal (vs WhatsApp).
- 50%+ of shopkeepers use the POS daily for at least one sale.
- Zero data corruption, less than 2 second load time on 4G.
