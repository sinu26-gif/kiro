Risk Register

  • LTP volatility risk — actual LTP on auction-close morning may differ; recompute bids.
  • Oversubscription risk — even a winning bid may receive only a fraction of target qty (proportional allotment).
  • Outlier discount risk — historic cohort had auctions that cleared >25% below LTP. Build buffer.
  • Listing-day price drop — the 30–60 day post-allotment window can erode profit margin.
  • Capital lock-up — bid amount is held until allotment+listing or refund (~7–15 business days).
  • Macro / budget shock — adverse policy moves on budget day can trigger sector-wide repricing.
  • Liquidity / circuit risk — small hydropower stocks can hit lower circuit, blocking exit.
  • Model risk — Monte Carlo assumes log-normal returns with optional jumps; tail risk may be underestimated.
  • Data quality risk — fundamentals/KPIs entered by hand; verify against company quarterly filings.
  • Bidding logistics — confirm DEMAT, broker auction bidding access and clear funds before auction open day.
